ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

Established: 1995. The Department provides an excellent educational environment that aims at bringing out the best. Our curriculum encompasses the recommendation of IEEE and ACM joint committee on CS Curriculum, National Curriculum Revision Committee, and duly approved by the HEC, MoE and MoST.

WHY STUDY WITH US

Our students get Unlimited access to high-end computing facilities. The institute department houses Over 10 teaching laboratories / interactive multimedia classrooms plus speedy Wi-Fi availability for students' use.
